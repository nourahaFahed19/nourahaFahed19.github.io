Our Story

Born from a love of
quiet beauty

Norah Jewelry was founded in Riyadh with a single belief: jewelry should feel as meaningful as it looks. We work only with precious metals and ethically sourced stones.

Our designs draw from the geometry of desert landscapes and the timeless vocabulary of fine jewelry.
